自我介绍

Good morning, teachers. I am really glad to be here for this interview. Now, please allow me to give a brief self-introduction.

My name is XXX, I come from Shuozhou, Shanxi Province, a beautiful city. and I graduated from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China, majoring in Software Engineering.

In general, I am a student who loves technology. Throughout my four years in university, I engaged in numerous engineering practices and completed many projects.During my college years, I worked in the studio and the lab center of the college of Software, and completed some web back-end projects and put them into practical use, and my engineering ability was recognized by the teachers of the college. Not only that, but I was also active in algorithmic competitions and learning the basics and won awards and scholarships.

If I am fortunate enough to be admitted, I will be honored to study under the guidance of the esteemed faculty members. I will undoubtedly dedicate myself to diligent study, actively practice what I learn, and strive to become a person of value to our nation and society. This concludes my self-introduction. Thank you for listening.

你的家乡

I'm from Shuozhou, Shanxi Province, and the local specialty is Knife-cut noodles. Shuozhou, historically known as Yanmen, is the hometown of Zhang Liao and Yu Chi Gong, two esteemed figures in Chinese history. Moreover, it is the ancestral home of Sa Zhenbing, a pivotal figure in China's naval history. The unique influence of Sa Zhenbing on the Chinese navy has sparked my interest in choosing your institution.

你的爱好

I enjoy reading, especially books on history and politics. I have a particular fascination with ancient Chinese history, so I often read classical Chinese literature. I believe that reading history can enhance my knowledge and inspire passion for life.My favorite book is "Records of the Three Kingdoms" by Chen Shou. This book provides a true account of the history of the Three Kingdoms period.

你的家庭

There are four members in my family: My parents, my cute dog and me. My father works at the railway company.He is often on duty. So most of the housework is done by my industrious mom. Climbing at weekends is our common interest. The fresh air and natural beauty can help us get rid of tiredness. They can strengthen our relation, too. During my preparing for coming here, my parents' love and support have always been my power. And I hope in future I will be able to repay them.

你的优缺点

My strengths are that I'm very studious and I can spend all day drilling a puzzle without getting bored.

My weakness is that I tend to be unconfident when doing things I'm not good at. I have recognized this problem and am in the process of correcting it.
